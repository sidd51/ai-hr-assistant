COMPANY EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT POLICY
======================================

1. ELIGIBLE EXPENSES
   The company reimburses expenses that are business-related and pre-approved.
   Categories include: travel, meals, accommodation, equipment, and training.

2. TRAVEL EXPENSES
   Economy class air travel is covered for trips under 6 hours.
   Business class is permitted for flights over 6 hours with manager approval.
   Local travel by cab or auto is fully reimbursable with receipts.
   Daily travel allowance: Rs. 500 within city, Rs. 1000 outside city.

3. MEAL EXPENSES
   Team meals are reimbursable up to Rs. 500 per person per meal.
   Client entertainment meals require manager pre-approval.
   Alcohol expenses are not reimbursable under any circumstances.

4. EQUIPMENT EXPENSES
   Work-from-home equipment up to Rs. 10,000 is reimbursable once per year.
   Includes: keyboard, mouse, monitor, USB hubs, webcam, headset.
   Receipts must be submitted within 30 days of purchase.

5. TRAINING AND COURSES
   Job-related courses and certifications are fully covered.
   Requires manager approval before enrollment.
   Employee must share a completion certificate with HR.

6. HOW TO SUBMIT AN EXPENSE CLAIM
   Submit claims through the HR Assistant with receipt photos.
   Claims must be submitted within 60 days of the expense.
   Reimbursement is processed within 5-7 working days after approval.
   Claims above Rs. 25,000 require Finance team approval.
