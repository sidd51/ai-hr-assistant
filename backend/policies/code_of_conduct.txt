COMPANY CODE OF CONDUCT
=========================

1. PROFESSIONAL BEHAVIOUR
   All employees must treat colleagues, clients, and vendors with respect.
   Harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any form is strictly prohibited.
   Employees must maintain confidentiality of company and client data.

2. WORKING HOURS
   Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
   Employees are expected to be available during core hours (10 AM - 4 PM).
   Remote work is permitted with manager approval up to 3 days per week.

3. CONFLICT OF INTEREST
   Employees must disclose any personal relationships with vendors or clients.
   Working for a competitor or starting a competing business is prohibited.
   Outside employment must be declared to HR and must not affect performance.

4. DATA AND SECURITY
   Company laptops must have full-disk encryption enabled at all times.
   Sharing login credentials with anyone, including teammates, is prohibited.
   Customer data must never be stored on personal devices.

5. SOCIAL MEDIA
   Employees must not share confidential company information on social media.
   Opinions expressed online must be clearly personal and not represent the company.

6. DISCIPLINARY PROCESS
   Violations are handled through a 3-step process: verbal warning, written
   warning, and termination. Severe violations may result in immediate termination.
   All disciplinary actions are documented and stored confidentially by HR.
