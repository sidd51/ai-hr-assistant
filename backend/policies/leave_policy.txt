COMPANY LEAVE POLICY
=====================

1. ANNUAL LEAVE
   All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of annual leave per year.
   Leave must be applied at least 5 working days in advance.
   Unused annual leave can be carried forward up to 5 days to the next year.
   Annual leave cannot be encashed except upon resignation or termination.

2. SICK LEAVE
   Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year.
   A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days.
   Sick leave cannot be carried forward to the next year.

3. MATERNITY LEAVE
   Female employees are entitled to 90 days of paid maternity leave.
   Maternity leave can begin up to 4 weeks before the expected delivery date.
   Employees must notify HR at least 8 weeks before the intended start date.
   Maternity leave is available after completing 6 months of service.

4. PATERNITY LEAVE
   Male employees are entitled to 5 days of paid paternity leave.
   Must be taken within 30 days of the child's birth.
   Requires a birth certificate to be submitted to HR.

5. LEAVE WITHOUT PAY
   Employees may apply for unpaid leave for personal reasons.
   Maximum duration is 30 days per year.
   Requires manager and HR approval at least 2 weeks in advance.

6. HOW TO APPLY FOR LEAVE
   All leave requests must be submitted through the HR Assistant.
   Requests are reviewed within 2 working days.
   Emergency leave can be applied retroactively within 24 hours.
